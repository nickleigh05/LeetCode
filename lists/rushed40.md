This is the minimum viable set: one or two problems per pattern, each chosen to teach something the others don't. It won't make you fast, but it'll make sure nothing in an interview is completely unfamiliar. Don't grind variants here — solve each one, understand *why* the pattern works, then move on. Once these click, the natural next step is the full 300-problem list to build depth and speed.
